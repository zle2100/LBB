Most smart bulbs have some indication about what App to use with them. Unfortunately, many do not. I purchased each lightbulb without a clearly listed partner App from my sample to find out if their boxes provided any more information.

The bulbs highlighted in Red were all listed with Apps. Hover over them for more information and to compare them to the other bulbs.

Next, we will see which came with No Apps listed.

Six bulbs came with No App listed. These were: FidgetFidget Ocamo Houkiper LGIDTECH TOOGOO and Zeroid

Without a proprietary App, customers risk poorly functioning products, poor operations, and no accountability for data collection.

It is also likely not a coincidence that the four lowest priced bulbs sampled had no accompanying App.
